Missionaries

  • Saint Patrick
  • St. Dominic
  • Francis Xavier
  • Ignatius of Loyola
  • St. Junípero Serra
  • Saint Patrick (c. 385-461)

    Missionary to Ireland, credited with converting the Irish to Christianity.

  • St. Dominic (1170-1221)

    Founded the Dominican Order, sent missionaries worldwide.

  • Francis Xavier (1506-1552)

    Jesuit missionary, spread Christianity in Asia.

  • Ignatius of Loyola (1491-1556)

    Founder of the Jesuits, known for their global missionary work.

  • St. Junípero Serra (1713-1784)

    Founded missions in California, known for his work with Native Americans.
